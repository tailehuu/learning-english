## Lesson 2

### Communication

**Clarifying, elaborating meaning**

- Do you mean ...?
- So you're saying ...?
- The point is that ...
- So this means ...
- What do you mean by ...?
- Why are we doing this?

**Opinions and actions**

- I think it's a good idea to ...
- I was trying to ...
- If we do this, then ...
- I (really) wanted to ...

**Dreams, Hopes, and Ambitions**

- I'm hoping to ...
- One day, I'd love to ...
- It's my dream to ...
- I'm working on ...
- My dream job is ...

### Grammar

**As ... As**

We can use `as ... as` with adjectives and adverbs to show if two people or things is similar or not.

- He run as fast as I do
- Jim's not as experienced as Anna.
